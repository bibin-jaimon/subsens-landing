Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how SubSens, operated by [Your Full Legal Name] ("we", "our", or "us"), collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our iOS application ("SubSens" or "the App") and our website at subsens.app ("the Website"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. By using the App or Website, you agree to this policy.

Data Controller

The data controller for your personal data is SubSens, operated by [Your Full Legal Name]. For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at privacy@subsens.app.

Data We Collect

iOS App

  • Subscription data: Names, amounts, billing cycles, renewal dates, categories, payment methods, notes, and usage logs you enter are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data. It is never uploaded to our servers.
  • Purchase-related data: If you purchase SubSens Pro, Apple and RevenueCat process your Apple ID and transaction information to validate your subscription. We do not store your payment details.

Website

  • When you visit subsens.app, Google Fonts may receive your IP address to load fonts. We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising on the Website.

What we do not collect: We do not use analytics, tracking, or ads. We do not collect your subscription content for any purpose other than local storage on your device or, if you opt in, in your own iCloud account.

iCloud Sync

iCloud sync is optional and opt-in only. When you enable it during onboarding or in Settings, your subscription data syncs to your personal iCloud account via Apple CloudKit. We do not have access to your iCloud data. You can disable sync at any time in Settings (an app restart may be required).

On-Device AI (Sens)

Sens, our subscription insights feature, uses Apple Foundation Models that run entirely on your device. No subscription data is sent to external AI services or to our servers. Insights are generated locally using only the data stored on your device.

Third-Party Services

SubSens uses the following third parties:

  • Apple: App distribution (App Store) and in-app purchases. See Apple Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and entitlement checks for SubSens Pro. See RevenueCat Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts: Font loading on the Website. See Google Privacy Policy.

Legal Basis (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area and UK:

  • Contract: Processing purchase and subscription data to provide SubSens Pro.
  • Legitimate interest: Using necessary website fonts and maintaining security.

International Transfers

Apple and RevenueCat may process data in the United States. They maintain appropriate safeguards (including adequacy decisions and Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable) for international transfers. See their respective privacy policies for details.

Data Retention

  • Local data: You control data on your device. Uninstalling the App removes it from your device. iCloud data remains in your account until you delete it.
  • RevenueCat and Apple: Retention is governed by their policies.

Your Rights

GDPR (EU/EEA/UK): You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, data portability, object, and withdraw consent. You may lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

CCPA (California): You have the right to know what data we collect and to request deletion. We do not sell your personal data. There is no need to opt out of sale.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@subsens.app.

Children's Privacy

SubSens is not intended for children under 13 (US) or under 16 in certain jurisdictions. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us and we will delete it.

Cookies and Website Storage

The Website does not use tracking or advertising cookies. Google Fonts loads fonts and may set technically necessary cookies. We do not use non-essential cookies.
