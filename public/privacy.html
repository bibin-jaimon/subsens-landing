Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Introduction

SubSens ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

SubSens is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Subscription Data: All subscription information you enter is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data.
  • Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app (if you opt-in).
  • Purchase Information: If you purchase SubSens Pro, payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not store your payment information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the SubSens service
  • Improve and optimize the app experience
  • Process premium subscription purchases
  • Send you important updates about the app

Data Storage

Your subscription data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your data.

Third-Party Services

SubSens uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For managing premium subscriptions (if applicable)
  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and in-app purchases

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time through the app
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Export your data (feature available in the app)
  • Opt-out of analytics (if applicable)

Children's Privacy

SubSens is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
