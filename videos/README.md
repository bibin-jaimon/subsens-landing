# Demo Video

**Place your app demo video here:**

```
web-app/public/videos/demo.mp4
```

- **Filename:** `demo.mp4`
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264) recommended for broad compatibility
- **Aspect ratio:** 9:19.5 (phone portrait) fits the hero mockup
- **Length:** 10–30 seconds works well (loops)
- **Settings:** Muted, autoplay, loop — no audio needed

The video loads **lazily** when the hero scrolls into view. A placeholder image shows until the video is ready.
